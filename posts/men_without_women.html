Men Without Women

Haruki Murakami

Buy it on Amazon

The book contains 7 short stories in total. All of the stories are associated with romance. Just as the title suggests, the stories are of the same significance- how important women can be to men. Without women, men' S lives become painfully lonely. The writing is polished and the concept was soul-stirring to me. Oof, yet another novel living rent-free in my head. My rating for this unique book, Men Without Women, is a solid 9/10.

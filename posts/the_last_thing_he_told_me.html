The Last Thing He Told Me

Laura Dave

Buy it on Amazon

The story revolves around a family of three. Hannah, her husband Owen, and Owen's daughter Bailey. Hannah's husband vanishes after delivering a note to her, entreating her to protect Bailey. Hannah and Bailey together try to find out Owen's motive behind the note and his disappearance. The plot of this story may seem captivating but I did not find it as intriguing because the middle of the story was somewhat dull. I would give The Last Thing Не Told Me a 5.5/1O.

A Good Girl's Guide To Murder

Holly Jackson

Buy it on Amazon

The story features Pippa, an adamant teen who reopens a closed murder case of her neighbourhood for a school project. As she attempts at proving the proclaimed murderer innocent based on "instinctual" belief, many loopholes are found and it gets even more exhilarating. This story tailors a quite clever plot that was unexpected. The characters are well-written and everything fell right into place at the end, so A Good Girl's Guide to Murder gets a 7.5/IO from me.
